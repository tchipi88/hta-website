The Challenge

1 in 3 people in Africa has high blood pressure, more than 6 in 10 are not aware and only 7% are controlled.

High blood pressure – or Hypertension – is the is a major contributor to cardiovascular diseases and death. Hypertension is the single most important risk factor for stroke as well as key factor for heart disease.

M-THYPA combines evidence-based clinical programs, advanced analytics and human coaching to help people to reduce their blood pressure and their heart risk.

How we do it

m-THYPA is redesigning the management of individuals with chronic disease in Africa by accelerating their contact with health care providers, facilitating the implementation of lifestyle change and monitoring their health-related data to share it with their physician.

1. Comprehensive screening

Home based screening with m-THYPA, based on an evidence-based algorithm to facilitate early intervention and access to clinicians for patients at risk.

2. Early access to medical Doctors

Via m-THYPA, Doctors have access to patient’s screening and monitoring data to provide early and adapted intervention to reduce your heart risk.

3. Personalized health coaching and monitoring

Patients receive personalized health coaching and monitoring to drive behavior change.

4. Evidence-based training

m-THYPA enable access to evidence-based training to improve understanding of disease and build healthy habits.
